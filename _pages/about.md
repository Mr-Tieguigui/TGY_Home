<span class='anchor' id='about-me'></span>

<p><strong>Guiyao Tie</strong> is a researcher focusing on <strong>LLM evaluation, reasoning capability analysis, post-training optimization, and LLM agent security</strong>. In recent years, his work has covered <strong>self-correction benchmarks, prompt injection attacks, agent risk assessment and defense, and multimodal agent safety</strong>. He is interested in the full research pipeline from fundamental capability analysis to security-oriented deployment in real systems.</p>

<p><em>More updates will be added over time.</em></p>
