<span class='anchor' id='about-me'></span>

**Guiyao Tie** is a researcher focusing on **LLM evaluation, reasoning capability analysis, post-training optimization, and LLM agent security**. His recent work covers **self-correction benchmarks, prompt injection attacks, agent risk assessment and defense, and multimodal agent safety**. He is interested in the full research pipeline from fundamental capability analysis to security-oriented deployment in real systems.
